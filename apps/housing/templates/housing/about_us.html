JEK Development, LLC

Since 2010, JEK Development has been operating in various Chicago Neighborhoods to build and to renovate existings buildings to provide rental housing for the people of Chicago.


JEK residences have been both planned and built with our core values in mind. A balanced life between work, family, and play. A balanced life that takes advantage of our cities great public transportation and neighborhoods! One that allows you the opportunity to make the most of your time, stick to your budget, and to get the most of everything the city of Chicago has to offer.

Interested in living in one of our units? For more information, contact us or follow us on LinkedIn and Facebook!
